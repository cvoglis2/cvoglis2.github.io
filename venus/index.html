Venus

The vaping jewelry
  • Available colors: gold, green, pink, purple
  • Release date: 23 November 2015
  • Find a Store
  • Description
  • Specification

The Venus series is one of VOGLIS luxury e-cigarettes for ladies. It has a fashionable and elegant design. Coated with world famous Swarovski crystals. The diamond colors can be based on your dressing, occasion, interests and your beautiful mood. The crystals comes in four colors; gold, green, pink and purple. It has a 24k gold plating embellishment. The mouth drip tip is made of galvanized frosted copper texture, a very beautiful red color. It has also a very high class durable tank window. When you smoke, a LED will radiate a beautiful blue light.

The Kit comes in a beautiful gift box. The content of the Kit is;
  • 1 battery
  • 1 atomizer
  • 1 USB charger
  • The battery capacity is 150 mAh and the charging time is 1.5h. The battery is replaceable and thus extend the e-cigarette life time. One charge correspond to approximately 120 puffs.
